TENDER DOCUMENT
Project: Severn Water — Trunk Main Replacement, Microtunnelling Works
Contract Reference: SW-UTL-2024-112
Client: Severn Trent Water Ltd
Issue Date: 2 February 2024
Tender Deadline: 15 March 2024

1. INTRODUCTION
Severn Trent Water invites suitably qualified contractors to tender for microtunnelling works associated with the replacement of a 600mm diameter trunk water main. The works are located in Coventry city centre and involve crossing beneath three main roads, a railway line, and the River Sherbourne.

2. DESCRIPTION OF WORKS
The works comprise:
- Five microtunnel drives totalling 890 metres
- Pipe diameter: 900mm OD steel carrier pipe (600mm DI liner)
- Method: Slurry microtunnelling with remote-controlled MTBM
- Shaft construction: 6 launch shafts, 4 reception shafts (secant pile or sheet pile)
- Dewatering: Wellpoint system — expected yield 15 litres/second

Drive Schedule:
Drive 1: Corporation Street underpass — 95m, depth 6.5m
Drive 2: Ring Road underpass — 140m, depth 8.2m
Drive 3: Railway crossing (Network Rail possession required) — 220m, depth 11.5m
Drive 4: River Sherbourne crossing — 85m, depth 4.8m
Drive 5: Hales Street underpass — 350m, depth 7.1m

3. GROUND CONDITIONS
Made ground to 2.5m depth, Mercia Mudstone below, with sandstone bands at 6–9m depth. Groundwater table at 3.5m.

4. CONTRACT VALUE AND DURATION
Estimated contract value: £4,200,000 — £5,800,000
Contract duration: 14 months
Start date: June 2024

8. KEY RISKS
- Railway possession window constraints limiting drive 3 productivity

- Urban traffic impact of lane closures
